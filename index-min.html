Bio

Bio

My graphic design story isn't very interesting, unfortunately.

I was working in science and realised I wasn't enjoying it anymore. I knew I wanted to do something creative, so I enrolled to study Web Dev. Part of the course was graphic design and eventually I realized I'm quite fond of it.

I now keep an eye on the design blogs and get great joy from all types of design; graphic, room design, typographic, architectural, environmental, etc.

I'm originally from Sydney, Australia and still live and work here.
